BRIEF HISTORY

Gsekure started as a small IT company in 2011 offering services in software/web development, networking, CCTV and surveillance equipment installations. We realised the urgent need of businesses looking to protect their sensitive data, to adhere to regulatory compliance, and reduce the risk posed by cybercriminals, Today, we are one of the world's fastest-growing cybersecurity companies, offering Incident Response, Ransomware Remediation, Managed Security Services, Virtual Information Security Offices, Security Operations, Cybersecurity Consulting, and an array of Cyber Compliance Services. Our objective centers on the success of each customer. Join hundreds of Gsekure customers who count on us to ensure cybersecurity and compliance success.
