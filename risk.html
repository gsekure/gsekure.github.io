FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. What are the system requirements for this Cyber Security Training Course?

The system requirement for this cybersecurity online course is:

  • System with an Intel i3 processor or above
  • Minimum 4GB RAM
  • Operating system can be of 32bit or 64 bit.

2. What are the prerequisites for this course?

This course assumes that you have a little background in networking or have taken the introductory cyber course offered by Gsekure academy. Its highly recommmended that you take the introductory cyber course before moving on to this course. However, difficult concept pertaining to the course will be explained.

3. How will I execute the Practicals in this Cyber security Certification Course?

The practicals will be executed on Windows and Linux Vm. A step by step set up guide is provided for configuring the environment.

4. What if I have queries after I complete this cybersecurity course?

Your access to the Support Team is for lifetime and will be available 24/7. The team will help you in resolving queries, during and after the cyber security course.
